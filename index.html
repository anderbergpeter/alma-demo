FAQ
What is ALMA and how does it differ from ChatGPT? ▾
ALMA is an enterprise AI assistant built on top of leading language models (GPT, Claude, Gemini and more), but connected directly to your company's internal systems — Jira, Confluence, Slack, Gmail, ERP and more. Unlike ChatGPT, every answer is grounded in your actual company data with a full audit trail.
Is my data safe? Does ALMA store my conversations? ▾
All data stays within your company's cloud environment. ALMA does not send data to third-party AI providers without encryption and anonymisation. Conversations are stored in your own tenant for audit purposes only, and admins can configure retention policies.
What are AI Colleagues and how do I choose the right one? ▾
AI Colleagues are pre-configured AI assistants tuned for specific departments or tasks — Finance, Sales, HR, Legal and more. Each AI Colleague has access to the relevant internal systems for that domain. Choose the one that matches your current task, or use James for general queries.
Can I create my own AI Colleague? ▾
Yes. Click the "+" next to "My AI Colleagues" in the sidebar. You can give it a name, description, instructions, and connect it to any available MCP tools. Watch the "Building your own Custom AI Colleague" video above for a step-by-step walkthrough.
Which AI models does ALMA support? ▾
ALMA supports models from OpenAI (GPT-5.4, GPT-4.1, o3), Anthropic (Claude Opus 4.6, Claude Sonnet 4.6), Google (Gemini 2.5 Pro, Gemini 2.5 Flash) and Mistral (Mistral Large 3, Codestral 2.5). You can switch model at any time using the selector at the top of the screen.
How do I report a problem or suggest a new feature? ▾
Use the feedback button (✨) in the chat input bar, or contact your ALMA administrator. Feature requests are tracked in Jira under the ALMA project board. You can also reach the team in the #alma-feedback Slack channel.
