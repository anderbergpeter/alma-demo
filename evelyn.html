How I calculate this
Methodology & assumptions — tap to review
  • Hours saved = tasks completed (automatically classified into 17 task types) × calibrated time-saved multiplier per task type.
  • Multipliers are calibrated from 4 237 self-reported responses within your organization. I update them weekly. Responses outside the 10th–90th percentile are winsorized.
  • Interaction cost subtracted: actual time each user spent with ALMA is logged and deducted from gross hours saved.
  • Realized value = hours saved × loaded personnel cost (SEK 850/h blended; you can set per-role rates) × 40% time-to-cash conversion (CFO-defensible baseline; recoverable time ≠ cash unless headcount changes).
  • Forecast = trailing 7-day trend, seasonality-adjusted, projected to month end.
  • Conservative by design. True realized value is likely higher. I'd rather under-sell and be trusted than over-sell once.
